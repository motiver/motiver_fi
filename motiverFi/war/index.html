Motiver is training and nutrition diary. It is designed especially for gym trainees and to support sport specific training. Intelligent tracking reports helps you reach the desired goals.

Data
The data Motiver will produce can be used to track personal progress easily. With different analysis reports you can set a clear goal, which encourages and motivates towards better results.

Friends
With FRIENDS feature you can share workouts, diets and personal results with your friends, which makes exercising more fun. COACH feature makes possible to allow to share your profile with desired personal, for example with your persoanl trainer. With this your trainer can easily create workout plan and goals for you.

Account
Using Motiver is easy and FREE. You can use your Google account to sign in, so no extra registration is needed.
